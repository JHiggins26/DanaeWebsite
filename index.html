About Vetta

Founder and CEO of Write It Out Publishing LLC est. 2017, Danae Daniels. Author name Vetta Shantell who was raised in East Baltimore, born Dec 23 and currently resides in Atlanta, Georgia.

Writing has always been her scape goat and looked at as just a hobby. Growing up she had many diaries, written poems, short stories, and her parents had her constantly doing book reports outside of school. If it involved a pen and some paper, she was down for the writing. With that being said, while on this journey of becoming an author, Vetta decided to self-publish and create her own publishing company that will create opportunities for not just her, but others as well.

She didn’t finish her first book until 2012, which hasn’t yet been published. Instead, she decided for her first book to be a children’s book for all ages titled, “Little Black Girl.” Choosing this book to be her first book was made so that ones interested in write it out publishing’s work and ideas will always know and understand her mindset regardless off what she puts out. Also, more books that are specifically for little black girls are needed.

Even though her mother says growing up she spoke of being a successful author, to her remembrance, she never thought of publishing anything and it made her nervous just to think about it until now. So, after all the anxiety, she felt confident enough that others may relate to her and the movement while also encouraging others to trust their process as well.
